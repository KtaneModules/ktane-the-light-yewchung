On the Subject of The Psychic Light

Are you certain this is the right light?

See Appendix: The Light of The Light for help identifying the various versions of The Light.

This module consists of an inactive light that lights up in one of Red, Green, Blue, or White when hovered. If this isn't the case, you're looking at a different module.

At each stage, you need to input a number of taps from 1 to 4, corresponding with one of four colors, depending on the display color and your previous answers. An incorrect answer will give a strike, but you'll remain on the same stage.

To get the color assignments, start with the ordering RGBW. Then, starting with the sum of port plates and battery holders, perform a number of swap operations on the table below equal to the number of numbers in your Serial Number, moving down the list, cycling back to 0 when you go above 7.

Then after every stage, move down a number of times equal to the inputted number, and perform just that operation to get your colors for the next stage. The module is complete after 5 stages.

0Reverse the ordering
1Swap 1 and 3
2Move 4 to the beginning
3Swap 3 and 4
4Swap 1 and 2
5Swap 12 and 34
6Swap 2 and 3
7Move 1 to the end

Stage 1:

If the display is R, input G.
If the display is G, input B.
If the display is B, input W.
If the display is W, input R.

Appendix: The Psychic Light

This appendix is for identifying variants of The Psychic Light.

Note the color displayed when you hover the Light, then unhover and rehover the Light. Does the color change?

  1. If no, consult the options below:
    1. If the displayed color is one of Red, Green, Blue, or White, you are looking at The Psychic Light.
    2. If the displayed color is one of Cyan, Magenta, Yellow, or Black, you are looking at Not The Psychic Light.
  2. If yes, consult the options below depending on how the color changes:
    1. If the displayed color cycles between Red, Green, Blue, and White, in any order, you are looking at The Reverse Psychic Light.
    2. If the displayed color cycles between any 4 different colors not including White, you are looking at The Complex Psychic Light.
    3. If the displayed color cycles between 8 different colors, you are looking at The Cruel Psychic Light.
